# Support

Contact

For login, account deletion, safety, privacy, billing, or gameplay problems, submit a support request. Include the device model, iPadOS or browser version, approximate time of the problem, and steps to reproduce it. Do not include passwords, access tokens, or private authentication codes.

[Open a support request](https://github.com/junaili/chess/issues/new)

For immediate player-safety concerns, block the player in the match screen and submit a report before leaving the match.
