# Support

Contact

For login, account deletion, safety, privacy, billing, or gameplay problems, email support. Include the device model, iPadOS or browser version, approximate time of the problem, and steps to reproduce it. Do not include passwords, access tokens, or private authentication codes.

[Email Jun at jun@accelbyte.ai](mailto:jun@accelbyte.ai)

For immediate player-safety concerns, block the player in the match screen and submit a report before leaving the match. Reports are recorded in our game-safety system and valid reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
